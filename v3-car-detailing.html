Paint Analysis Before Every Job

Paint depth is measured with a calibrated gauge before anything is touched. This determines exactly how much correction is safe and what the finish can achieve — no guessing, no over-cutting, no over-promising.

📸

Full Before & After Documentation

Every vehicle is photographed under inspection lighting before and after the job. You'll receive a digital report showing exactly what was corrected. No guesswork, no trust-me — just documented proof of the result.

📜

Written Warranties on All Coatings

Every ceramic coating and PPF installation comes with a written warranty — not just a handshake. Gyeon coatings are independently warranted through the manufacturer, so your protection is backed by more than just us.

🔒

Your Vehicle Never Leaves the Studio Unattended

All work is carried out in a locked, climate-controlled studio in Marrickville. Your vehicle is never driven unnecessarily, never parked outside, and only handled by the person doing the work.
